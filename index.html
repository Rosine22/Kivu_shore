Entertainment & Travel

Jet skiing is one of the most popular water sport we have. It involves riding a small motorized watercraft on water water bodies. They are usualy ridden by one or two people and offer an exciting way to explore the water and enjoy high-speed thrills.
Fishing: This activity is usually done to specific places of lake Kivu where fishes and other aqcuatic animals are concentrated. We offer all the materials needed and those who need to learn fishing are trained by experts to make this activity enjoyable for everyone.
Kayaking is a versatile and enjoyable outdoor activity that allows people to connect with nature, explore waterways, and develop paddling skills. Whether you're looking for a relaxing day on a calm lake or an adrenaline-pumping whitewater adventure, canoeing offers a variety of experiences for outdoor enthusiasts in Lake Kivu.
A trip to Bralirwa. Bralirwa (Brasseries et Limonaderies du Rwanda) is a Rwandan beverage company that specializes in the production and distribution of a variety of alcoholic and non-alcoholic beverages. The company is known for its prominent presence in the Rwandan market and its role in providing a range of beverages to consumers in Rwanda.
Live band activity is the performance and musical presentation of a group of musicians playing instruments and singing together in front of a live audience. It typically involves a combination of vocals, guitars, drums, keyboards, bass, and other instruments, creating a dynamic and energetic musical experience.
